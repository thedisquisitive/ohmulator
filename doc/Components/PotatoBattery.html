Potato Battery

A Potato Battery generates electrical energy through the chemical reaction between the potato's acid and two electrodes.

Overview

Potato batteries are similar to lemon batteries, demonstrating how chemical energy can be converted to electrical energy. They use the starches and acids in potatoes to generate a small current.

Methods

  • getName() const - Returns the name of the battery.

Example


// Create a Potato Battery
PotatoBattery batt;
std::cout << "Battery: " << batt.getName() << std::endl;

NiCad Battery

A NiCad (Nickel-Cadmium) Battery is a rechargeable battery known for its durability and ability to withstand many charge cycles.

Overview

NiCad batteries are durable and capable of enduring many charge cycles. They have been widely used in portable electronics but have been mostly replaced by other types of rechargeable batteries.

Methods

  • getName() const - Returns the name of the battery.

Example


// Create a NiCad Battery
NiCadBattery batt;
std::cout << "Battery: " << batt.getName() << std::endl;

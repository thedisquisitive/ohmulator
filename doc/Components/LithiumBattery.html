Lithium Battery

A Lithium Battery is a rechargeable battery known for its high energy density and long lifespan.

Overview

Lithium batteries are used in many portable devices due to their long life and ability to store large amounts of energy. They are rechargeable and can provide consistent voltage over multiple charge cycles.

Methods

  • getName() const - Returns the name of the battery.

Example


// Create a Lithium Battery
LithiumBattery batt;
std::cout << "Battery: " << batt.getName() << std::endl;

Code of Conduct

Ohmulator is an open-source project for learning and fun, and we want to make sure it stays that way for everyone! We ask everyone in the community to be respectful, kind, and helpful.

Our Promise

We, as members of the Ohmulator community, pledge to make this project a safe and welcoming space for everyone, no matter their background or experience level. Whether you're a student, a hobbyist, or just curious about electronics, you're welcome here!

What We Expect

We expect community members to:

  • Be friendly and respectful in discussions, whether they’re on GitHub, in comments, or elsewhere.
  • Help others if you can. We're all here to learn!
  • Accept feedback gracefully and provide feedback kindly.
  • Keep things fun and enjoyable for everyone.

What’s Not Cool

Here are a few things that aren’t okay:

  • Being rude or disrespectful to others.
  • Posting offensive, inappropriate, or harmful content.
  • Making anyone feel unwelcome or uncomfortable.

If Things Go Wrong

If someone is behaving inappropriately or making the project less fun for others, you can report it by contacting us at contact@ohmulator.org. We'll do our best to address the situation and make sure everyone feels welcome.

It's All About Fun and Learning

Ohmulator is a casual, fun project, so let's keep the spirit light and positive. Mistakes happen, and that’s okay — we’re all learning together!
